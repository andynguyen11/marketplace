How Loom Works

Our goal is to provide meaningful connections
between freelancers and entrepreneurs
who want to trade cash and equity for work.

We're all about bringing great ideas to life.

  • For Entrepreneurs
  • For Freelancers

Post your project

Create a profile for yourself, provide details about your company, then tell the community about your project and the work you need done. Include up to 3 custom questions for each freelancer to answer in their proposal.

Receive proposals

Developers from around the world will submit proposals with equity and/or cash bids to work on your project. Respond or decline proposals to let the freelancer know if you're interested.

Engage top freelancers

View profiles and send up to 5 messages to each freelancer for free. If you want to continue the discussion after 5 messages, you may request a connection and pay to further engage them.

Create your profile

Upload a photo of yourself, tell hiring-entrepreneurs a bit about you, add work examples and employment history, and take skill tests to show off your technical skills. The more info you can provide, the more likely you'll be to win projects.

Submit proposals

Find a project that matches your skills and submit a proposal explaining why you're the right person for the gig. Entrepreneurs will respond with a message or decline the proposal so you know if they're interested.

Get hired on great projects

When an entrepreneur responds to your proposal we provide five free messages to discuss project details. After five messages, they can request a connection with you to exchange contact details and hire you for the project.
