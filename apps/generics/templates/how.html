This is how
Loom works

Post your project

Easily create a profile for yourself or your company and tell the community about the project you are bringing to life. Describe the basics of the project on your public page and keep your confidential details hidden behind an NDA-protected tab.

You can grant developers access to your confidential info by having them agree to an NDA. On Loom, you can garner interest from the development community without spilling your secret sauce.

Receive bids from developers

Developers from around the world will place bids in equity and/or cash to build your project, ultimately setting the price of the work in true marketplace fashion. We share the average bid placed on a project with the community to help developers gauge what type of offers are coming in (and outbid them).

Engage the developer of your choice

Easily engage any developer that has bid on your project to bring it to life, using our complimentary industry-standard digital contracts.

Sort and filter all of the bids from developers to build your project based on cash offers, equity offers, technical proficiency, user ratings, location and more. When you pay to engage a developer on Loom, the small fee includes all legal contracts through the professional, industry-standard templates to be signed digitally by both parties. Once both parties agree sign, we provide you with your developer’s personal information to contact them however you both see fit, as well as to invite them to your team’s Slack, JIRA, etc organizations to begin working.
