This is how
Loom works

Post your project

Easily create a profile for yourself or your company and tell the community about the project you are bringing to life. Tell the basics of the project on your public page and keep your confidential details hidden behind an NDA-protected tab.

You can grant developers access to your confidential info by having them agree to an NDA as you see fit. On Loom, you can garner interest from the development community without spilling your secret sauce.

Receive bids from developers

Developers from around the world will place bids in equity and/or cash to build your project, ultimately setting the price of the work in true marketplace fashion. The current lowest bid is made available for the community to see (and outbid). When you post your project on Loom, you may be surprised at what comes back to you.
